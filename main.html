Paris


Paris, known as the "City of Light," is the capital of France and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its iconic landmarks, charming streets, and vibrant lifestyle make it a dream destination for travelers around the world.

  1. Eiffel Tower: A world-famous landmark offering breathtaking views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum: Home to iconic artworks like the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral: A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées: A bustling avenue with shops, cafes, and the Arc de Triomphe.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica: A beautiful church perched on Montmartre hill.
  • Croissants: Buttery, flaky pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Macarons: Colorful and delicate cookies filled with ganache or cream.
  • Escargot: A traditional dish made with snails and garlic butter.
  • Ratatouille: A vegetable stew that's a staple of French cuisine.
  • Crème Brûlée: A creamy dessert with a caramelized sugar topping.
